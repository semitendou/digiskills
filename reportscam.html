Report a Scam

If you've encountered a scam, we're here to help. Report it and get resources to protect yourself and others.

⚠️ Important: You are not alone

Scams happen to millions of people every year, and it's never your fault. If you've fallen victim to a scam, taking action now can help protect you and others. The sooner you report it, the better.

What to Document

Before reporting, gather as much information as possible:

  • Names/usernames of scammers
  • Email addresses or phone numbers used
  • Website URLs involved
  • Dates and times of contact
  • Screenshots of messages
  • Amount of money lost (if applicable)
  • What information was shared
  • How you were contacted

Report to Digital Skills for All

Share your experience with us. This helps us create better educational content and warn our community about emerging scams.

Thank you for reporting! Your information will help us protect others.

⚠️ Important Safety Tips After Being Scammed:

  • Change your passwords immediately for all accounts
  • Monitor your bank and credit card statements
  • Consider placing a fraud alert with your credit bureau
  • Do not send any more money or information to the scammer
  • Block the scammer on all platforms
  • Save all evidence (screenshots, emails, messages)
  • Talk to someone you trust about what happened
