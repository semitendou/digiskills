Our Mission

Building a digitally confident and connected community where everyone can thrive safely online.

Mission Statement

"To empower individuals of all ages and backgrounds with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to navigate the digital world safely, critically, and effectively, fostering inclusive communities where technology enhances rather than intimidates."

Key Objectives

Reduce Digital Divide

Bridge the gap between different age groups and communities in digital literacy and online confidence.

Prevent Online Harm

Protect communities from scams, misinformation, and digital threats through proactive education.

Build Confidence

Transform digital anxiety into digital confidence through practical, hands-on learning experiences.

Foster Connection

Use technology to strengthen community bonds and facilitate meaningful connections across generations.

Promote Independence

Enable seniors and vulnerable populations to maintain independence through digital tools and services.

Cultivate Responsibility

Develop responsible digital citizens who contribute positively to online communities and discourse.

Our Vision

We envision a New Zealand where every person, regardless of age or background, can participate fully and confidently in the digital world. A nation where grandparents video chat with grandchildren without anxiety, where young people critically evaluate online information, and where communities use technology to strengthen bonds rather than create barriers. We see a future where digital literacy is not a source of division, but a bridge connecting generations and empowering all citizens to thrive in our increasingly connected world.
