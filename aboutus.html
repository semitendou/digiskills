About Digital Skills for All

Empowering digital confidence across generations through accessible, practical education and community support.

Our Story

Digital Skills for All was born from a simple observation: in our rapidly evolving digital world, too many people feel left behind or overwhelmed by technology. Whether it's seniors struggling with online banking, parents concerned about their children's digital safety, or young people navigating the complex landscape of social media and misinformation, we recognized a critical need for accessible, practical digital education.

Founded in 2024, our organization brings together educators, cybersecurity experts, digital natives, and community advocates who share a common vision: a world where everyone can navigate the digital landscape with confidence, safety, and purpose. We believe that digital literacy is not a luxury‚Äîit's an essential life skill that should be available to everyone, regardless of age, background, or experience level.

What started as small community workshops has grown into a comprehensive digital literacy platform serving thousands of learners across New Zealand. Our approach is unique because we don't just teach technical skills‚Äîwe build confidence, foster critical thinking, and create supportive communities where learners can grow together.

As technology continues to evolve, so do we. We're constantly developing new resources, updating our curriculum, and finding innovative ways to reach learners where they are.
