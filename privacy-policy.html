Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy and personal information is our top priority.

Our Privacy Commitment

At Digital Skills for All, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our website, attend our workshops, or interact with our services.

We believe that privacy is a fundamental right, and we handle your personal information with the utmost care and respect. We only collect information that is necessary to provide our services, and we never sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services:

Contact Information

Name, email address, phone number when you contact us or register for workshops

Workshop Participation

Attendance records, feedback forms, and learning progress (anonymized for reporting)

Website Usage

Cookies, IP address, browser type, and pages visited (for improving our website)

Communication Records

Emails, messages, and feedback you send to us

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for legitimate purposes related to our educational mission:

  • Service Delivery: To provide workshops, resources, and support services
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and send important updates
  • Improvement: To analyze and improve our programs and website
  • Safety: To protect against fraud and ensure the security of our services
  • Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory requirements

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and only share information in limited circumstances:

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to anyone
  • Share your information for marketing purposes
  • Provide your data to advertisers

We MAY share information when:

  • Required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • Protecting the safety and rights of our users
  • With your explicit consent
  • With trusted service providers who help us operate our website (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Personal information is stored on secure servers
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access personal data
  • Regular Updates: We keep our security measures up to date

Data Retention

We only keep your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. When we no longer need your information, we securely delete or anonymize it.

  • Contact Information: Retained while you're actively using our services
  • Website Analytics: Aggregated data kept for statistical purposes

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how people use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for learners of all ages, including children under 13. We take additional precautions to protect children's privacy:

  • We obtain parental consent before collecting information from children under 13
  • We limit the personal information we collect from children
  • Parents can review, correct, or delete their child's information at any time
  • We don't share children's information with third parties

Contact Us About Privacy

If you have questions about this privacy policy, want to exercise your privacy rights, or have concerns about how we handle your personal information, please contact us. We're here to help and take all privacy concerns seriously.
